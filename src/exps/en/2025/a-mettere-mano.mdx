Abitare il conflitto - “Occupare l’immaginario” at Cascina Occupata Torchiera (Milano)

In the tension between preserving and forgetting, what is the role of movement archives? How can we preserve the memory of conflicts? What conflictuality exists in archiving?

10:30 AM - 12:30 PM Discussion with: Collettiva Interzona – Bibliotork Interzona Caronia (Milano), Archivio Primo Moroni (Milano), APE (Milano), Archivio proletario internazionale (Milano), Centro Studi Libertari – Archivio Pinelli (Milano), Laboratorio Intergalattico Giacomo Verde Artivista (Viareggio), REBAL, Grafton9 (Bologna), Italian Tapes Archive (Milano), and anyone else who wishes to join.
